# Suggested 7-day follow-up sequence

## Day 0

Send cold email and LinkedIn connection request.

## Day 2 or 3

Send Follow-up 1.

## Day 6 or 7

Send Follow-up 2.

## If they reply interested

Send:

```text
Great. For the sample, send me:

1. client niche
2. market/geography
3. 2 to 4 competitors
4. the client goal, such as rankings, reviews, content, PR, or client reporting
5. whether this should be internal-only or client-forwardable

I will build the sample as a reviewed packet with source links, evidence notes, caveats, and QA status.
```

## If they ask price

Send:

```text
For the first pilot I would keep it simple: $500 for one month, one client niche, one weekly brief, source appendix and QA report included. Your team reviews every packet before anything goes to a client.
```

## If they ask if it is AI

Send:

```text
The value is not generic AI summarization. The value is the source structure, evidence notes, client-safe caveats, recommended actions, and reviewable delivery packet. It is built so your agency can quickly decide what is useful and what is safe to forward.
```
