# Cold Outreach Email

## Primary email

Subject: White-label client briefs for your agency

Hi {{first_name}},

I am building a white-label weekly intelligence brief for agencies that already manage SEO, PR, content, reputation, or client reporting retainers.

The idea is simple: your team sends me one client niche, a few competitors, and the market you care about. I turn public-source monitoring and manual analyst notes into a clean weekly brief with:

- top client-relevant developments
- competitor and reputation signals
- recommended client actions
- source links, evidence notes, and QA status
- a delivery packet your team can review, edit, and forward

This is not a SaaS dashboard or an AI content dump. It is a reviewed client intelligence layer your agency can use to create better check-in conversations and sharper reporting.

I am looking for a few agencies to test the first paid pilot. If you send me one client niche, I can build a sample brief so you can see exactly what it would look like for your agency.

Worth a quick look?

Best,
{{your_name}}

## Personalization hooks

Use one of these in the first line when possible:

- I noticed your agency works with local service businesses.
- I saw you publish client case studies around SEO and reputation.
- I noticed your team does content strategy and ongoing reporting.
- I saw your agency serves multi-location businesses.
- I noticed you work with founder-led or local businesses where client education matters.

## Call-to-action options

Use one clear CTA:

- Want me to build a sample for one client niche?
- Would a sample brief be useful?
- Is there one client niche you would want to test this on?
- Worth a quick look?
