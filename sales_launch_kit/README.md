# Brief Factory v0.4 Sales Launch Kit

This folder contains the sales assets for launching Brief Factory as a service-first white-label agency brief product.

## Goal

Get the first paid pilot by selling a simple offer:

```text
Send me one client niche and I will build a sample weekly intelligence brief your agency can review.
```


## How to use

1. Pick one agency type.
2. Pick one client niche.
3. Send the cold email or LinkedIn DM.
4. Offer one custom sample brief.
5. Use the onboarding questionnaire to collect the niche, client, competitors, service area, and source preferences.
6. Run Brief Factory with a custom client config, source registry, and manual intake file.
7. Send the sample packet and ask for a paid one-month pilot.

## Best first buyer

Start with agencies that already sell monthly retainers:

```text
local SEO agencies
reputation management agencies
PR firms
content marketing agencies
fractional CMO shops
small B2B marketing agencies
```

Do not sell this as software yet. Sell it as a reviewed weekly client intelligence layer that saves research time and improves client conversations.
