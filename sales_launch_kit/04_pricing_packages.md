# Pricing Packages

Use these as starting prices. The first goal is to close a paid pilot, learn the fulfillment load, and tighten the offer.

## Paid sample

Price: $150 to $300 one-time

Best for:

- agencies that are interested but cautious
- first cold prospects
- custom proof-of-concept briefs

Includes:

- one client niche
- one sample brief
- 3 to 6 researched items
- source appendix
- QA report
- 1 revision pass

Use when:

```text
The agency has not seen the product yet and wants a low-risk sample.
```

## Starter monthly pilot

Price: $500/month

Best for:

- one agency
- one client niche
- one weekly brief

Includes:

- 4 weekly briefs per month
- up to 5 top items per brief
- source appendix
- QA report
- handoff email
- one monthly improvement note

Use when:

```text
The agency wants to test Brief Factory with one active client.
```

## Agency three-client pack

Price: $1,500/month

Best for:

- agencies with multiple clients in similar niches
- local SEO or reputation agencies
- content agencies with recurring reporting needs

Includes:

- up to 3 client briefs per week
- shared source framework where useful
- client-specific competitor/watchlist fields
- weekly delivery packets
- monthly source-quality review

Use when:

```text
The agency already has repeatable retainers and wants a margin-expanding reporting layer.
```

## Premium agency desk

Price: $2,500 to $3,500/month

Best for:

- PR firms
- B2B agencies
- agencies with complex accounts
- agencies that want deeper client strategy notes

Includes:

- up to 5 client briefs per week
- custom source registry
- deeper evidence notes
- competitive signal tracker
- monthly strategy call
- priority turnaround

Use when:

```text
The agency wants Brief Factory to operate like a research back office.
```

## Do not underprice these parts

Charge more when the client wants:

- multiple verticals
- daily briefs
- heavy manual research
- exact competitor tracking across many brands
- custom formatting
- client-specific calls
- rush turnaround
- regulated or legal-sensitive topics

## Pricing rule

Do not sell broad monitoring. Sell a narrow deliverable.

Good:

```text
One weekly local SEO intelligence brief for one roofing client in Toronto.
```

Bad:

```text
We will monitor everything in your industry.
```

## First close target

The first real target is either:

```text
2 clients at $500/month
```

or:

```text
1 agency at $1,000/month
```

The goal is not perfect scale yet. The goal is proof that agencies will pay for reviewed brief packets.
