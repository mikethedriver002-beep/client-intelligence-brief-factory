# LinkedIn DM Scripts

## Connection request

Hi {{first_name}}, I am building a white-label weekly client intelligence brief for agencies that manage SEO, PR, reputation, or content retainers. Thought it might be relevant to your work with {{agency_focus}}.

## DM after connection

Thanks for connecting, {{first_name}}.

Quick context: I am testing a white-label brief product for agencies.

You send one client niche, competitors, and market. I send back a weekly intelligence packet with the top client-relevant signals, recommended actions, source links, evidence notes, and QA status.

It is meant to help agencies create better client check-ins without hiring another researcher.

I can build a sample for one client niche if you want to see the format.

## Short DM

I am testing a white-label weekly intelligence brief for agencies. It turns public-source monitoring plus analyst notes into a client-ready packet with signals, recommended actions, sources, and QA notes.

Want me to make a sample for one client niche?

## Follow-up DM

Circling back, {{first_name}}. The sample offer is simple: send me one client niche and 2 to 4 competitors, and I will show you what a weekly client intelligence packet could look like for your agency.

No dashboard. No new tool to learn. Just a reviewed packet your team can use or adapt.

## Voice rules

Keep DMs:

- short
- specific
- service-first
- not hypey
- not AI-focused
- focused on better client conversations

Avoid:

- promising full automation
- saying the brief replaces analysts
- pitching a platform before showing the sample
- long paragraphs
