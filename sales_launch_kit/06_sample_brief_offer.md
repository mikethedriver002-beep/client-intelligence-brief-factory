# Sample Brief Offer

## Core offer

Send me one client niche and I will build a sample weekly intelligence brief your agency can review.

## What the agency sends

The agency sends:

```text
client niche
client website or anonymized description
market or geography
2 to 4 competitors
3 to 5 watch topics
what the client cares about most
```

## What they receive

The agency receives a sample packet with:

```text
weekly_brief.md
weekly_brief.html
outreach_ready_sample.md
agency_sales_sample.md
source_appendix.csv
qa_report.md
delivery_manifest.json
handoff_email.md
```

## How to position the sample

This is not a free custom strategy project. It is a proof-of-concept showing the agency what a recurring brief would look like.

Use this line:

```text
I will build one sample so you can judge the format, evidence quality, and usefulness before we talk about a monthly pilot.
```

## Paid sample framing

Recommended paid sample price:

```text
$150 to $300
```

Position it as:

```text
A low-risk custom sample for one client niche.
```

## Free sample framing

Use a free sample only if the agency is a strong fit.

Rules for free samples:

- one niche only
- no more than 3 items
- no revision pass unless they book a pilot call
- no guarantee of ongoing monitoring
- sample can be anonymized
