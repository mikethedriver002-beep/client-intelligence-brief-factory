# First 25-Prospect Target List Structure

The first 25 prospects should not be random agencies. They should be agencies that already sell recurring services where a weekly intelligence brief makes sense.

## Prospect mix

Target 25 prospects like this:

```text
10 local SEO or reputation agencies
5 PR firms
5 content marketing agencies
3 fractional CMO or small strategy shops
2 niche newsletter or creator-ops teams
```

## Must-have fit signals

Prioritize prospects with at least two of these:

- clear monthly retainer services
- case studies or client results
- local SEO, reputation, PR, or content services
- works with local businesses, professional services, home services, healthcare, SaaS, or B2B clients
- posts insights or client education content
- shows founder or partner contact information
- smaller team where research support may be useful

## Avoid for now

Avoid prospects that are:

- giant agencies with long buying processes
- pure paid-media shops with no client strategy layer
- agencies with no visible retainers or reporting work
- agencies serving only huge enterprise accounts
- agencies that appear inactive

## Prospect scoring

Score each prospect from 1 to 5.

```text
5 = perfect fit, send personalized outreach
4 = strong fit, send outreach
3 = maybe, use later
2 = weak fit, skip for now
1 = bad fit
```

## First 25 workflow

1. Find 25 agencies.
2. Score them.
3. Pick the top 10.
4. Personalize each first line.
5. Send 5 cold emails per day.
6. Send LinkedIn connection requests to the same people.
7. Offer one sample brief.
8. Track every reply.
9. Convert interested replies into sample requests.
10. Convert useful sample feedback into a one-month paid pilot.

## Best first sample niches

Use niches where the value is easy to understand:

- roofing companies
- dental clinics
- med spas
- personal injury law firms
- HVAC companies
- home remodeling companies
- local restaurant groups
- boutique fitness studios
- real estate brokerages
- accounting firms

## Daily outreach target

Start small:

```text
5 personalized emails per day
5 LinkedIn connection requests per day
1 sample brief offer per interested reply
```

The goal is not volume first. The goal is learning which agency type reacts.
